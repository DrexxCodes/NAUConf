Abstract Submission Guidelines

Please review and follow these guidelines carefully for your submission

Abstract Requirements

  • Word Limit: Abstract should not exceed 250 words, clearly stating the research problem, objectives, methodology, and key findings or arguments.
  • Biography: Include a short biography (maximum 100 words) with your institutional affiliation and contact information. Making the total word count to be 350 words.

Full Paper Specifications

  • Length: Full papers should be between 5,000 and 7,000 words, including references.

Format & Style Requirements

  • File Format: Abstracts and papers should be submitted in Microsoft Word format, using APA 7th edition referencing style.

Submission Channels

All abstracts and papers should be sent to:

polscienceconference@conf.unizik.edu.ng
OR
